Panneer Pizza

Paneer pizza is a fusion dish that combines the Italian classic pizza with Indian flavors, featuring paneer cheese as a prominent ingredient. Paneer, a type of fresh cheese common in Indian cuisine, is often used in various dishes due to its mild flavor and ability to absorb spices well.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough or pre-made pizza crust
  • Paneer cheese, cubed or crumbled
  • Bell peppers (red, green, yellow), sliced
  • Onions, thinly sliced
  • Tomatoes, sliced
  • Olive oil (for brushing the crust)
  • Spices such as cumin, coriander, chili powder, and salt (to taste)
  • Fresh cilantro leaves (optional, for garnish)
  • Cheese (optional, for additional topping or layering)
  • Garlic, minced (optional, for added flavor)

Preparation

  1. Preheat your oven: Preheat your oven to the temperature specified for your pizza dough. It's typically around 450°F (230°C) for homemade pizzas.
  2. Prepare the pizza dough: If you're using homemade pizza dough, roll it out on a lightly floured surface into your desired shape and thickness. If you're using a pre-made pizza crust, you can skip this step.
  3. Prepare the toppings: Chop the bell peppers, onions, tomatoes, and any other vegetables you want to use. Cube or crumble the paneer cheese.
  4. Prepare the sauce: If you're making your own tomato sauce, you can cook it on the stove by simmering crushed tomatoes with garlic, onions, and spices until it thickens. Alternatively, you can use store-bought pizza sauce or marinara sauce
  5. Assemble the pizza: Place the rolled-out pizza dough on a baking sheet or pizza stone. Spread a layer of tomato sauce over the dough, leaving a small border around the edges for the crust. Sprinkle your desired spices over the sauce for added flavor.
  6. Add the toppings: Evenly distribute the chopped vegetables and paneer cheese over the sauce. You can also sprinkle some additional cheese on top if you like your pizza extra cheesy.
  7. Bake the pizza: Place the assembled pizza in the preheated oven and bake for about 12-15 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Finish and serve: Once the pizza is done baking, remove it from the oven and let it cool for a few minutes. Garnish with fresh cilantro leaves if desired. Slice the pizza and serve hot.

Tip

Use fresh paneer: Fresh paneer will have the best texture and flavor. If possible, make or buy paneer that is freshly made for optimal taste. Marinate the paneer: For added flavor, marinate the paneer cubes in a mixture of yogurt and spices for at least 30 minutes before using them as a pizza topping. This will infuse the paneer with extra taste and tenderness.
